About LaserPro Studio

I’m Brad, a Delaware County local, professional welder, and former welding instructor with years of hands-on experience. At LaserPro Studio, I bring my eye for precision and love for creative challenges to custom laser engraving and cutting. Whether it’s personalized gifts, awards and plaques, home décor, keepsakes, or something unique, I collaborate with my customers—gift givers, businesses, event planners, sports teams, and DIY’ers—to turn their ideas into high-quality, tailored creations.

This venture started as a way to stay productive during winter slowdowns (I’m not a fan of welding in the cold!). After working with lasers in my teaching days, I saw an opportunity to explore a new hobby, broaden my skills, and dive into online sales via Etsy. My background ensures every piece reflects top-notch craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a personal touch—delivering products that are as meaningful as the occasions they’re for, all built to last.
