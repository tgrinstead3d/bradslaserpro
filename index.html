About LaserPro Studio

I’m Brad—a local educator, welder, and all-around hands-on guy. At LaserPro Studio, I bring the precision of welding into custom laser engraving and cutting. Whether it’s personalized gifts, awards, home décor, or something completely unique, I turn ideas into high-quality, one-of-a-kind pieces.

After working with lasers while teaching, I turned it into another side hustle and built a home studio. Now, I work with gift-givers, businesses, and DIY’ers to create custom pieces for any occasion.

Got an idea or some Pinterest inspiration but not sure how to bring it to life? Let’s talk—I’m always up for a new project.
